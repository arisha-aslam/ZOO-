CENTRAL Zoo is a really big zoo that cares a lot about animals and teaching people. It started in 1980 on a small piece of land but got much bigger in 2000, now covering 33 acres. They want to keep lots of different animals and make sure they have good homes.

The zoo really cares about the animals and also wants people to be safe when they visit. They have strong rules to make sure everyone has a good and secure time. People who work at the zoo take special care of the animals, making sure they have the right food and checking on them often to keep them healthy.

CENTRAL Zoo is working on a website that will share cool information about the animals, like what kind they are and where they live in the zoo. You'll also get to see awesome pictures of the animals, even if you can't visit the zoo in person. It's like taking a virtual trip to learn about the amazing creatures that live at CENTRAL Zoo!
