Visitor Essentials

Discover affordable and convenient admission at Central Zoo. we strive to make your visit as accessible and convenient as possible. Admission rates are as follows: Adults (13-64) pay $5.19, Children (3-12) are $3.00, and Seniors (65+) pay $8.40. Kids under 3 enter free. Students enjoy discounts with a valid ID, and groups of 15+ get exclusive offers—reach out to our group sales.
